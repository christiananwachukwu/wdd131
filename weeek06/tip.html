Handwashing Guide

This is the proper way to wash your hands!

  1. Wet hands with clean and running water
  2. Apply soap and lather well
  3. Scrub all surfaces, including backs of hands, between fingers, and under nails, for at least 20 seconds
  4. Rinse thoroughly under running water
  5. Dry hands using a clean towel or air dry

Bravo! Your hands are sparkling clean!
